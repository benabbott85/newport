About Me

Passionate about building great web experiences

Developer, problem-solver, continuous learner.

I'm Ben Abbott, a full stack web developer based in Denver, Colorado. After transitioning from the construction industry, I pursued my passion for technology at the University of Denver, where I completed an intensive full stack web development bootcamp.

Since then, I've been teaching others how to write code, working with small design firms across the Denver metro, and most recently serving as Senior Web Developer at Red Rock Investor Technology, where I handle UI/UX development and investor portal integrations.

I'm driven by curiosity — I love understanding how things work under the hood and translating that understanding into clean, purposeful code. Whether it's front-end polish or back-end architecture, I'm committed to quality from first commit to final deploy.
